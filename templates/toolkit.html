Embedded Ethics Learning Modules

Guided modules based on the 5 E's of Constructivism — a pedagogical framework for embedding ethical reasoning into computing education.

1
Engage
Start with real-world AI ethics scenarios that connect to students' experiences and communities. Surface assumptions and spark curiosity.
2
Explore
Investigate AI systems hands-on — examine datasets, test models, and discover how technical decisions create ethical implications.
3
Explain
Articulate ethical frameworks and principles. Students develop vocabulary and reasoning tools for responsible AI development.
4
Elaborate
Apply ethical reasoning to complex, open-ended AI challenges. Design solutions that center equity, privacy, and community impact.
5
Evaluate
Reflect on learning and assess AI systems against ethical criteria. Develop rubrics and assessment tools for responsible practice.
Developed through NSF CUE-M Grant #2434338 at CAHSI institutions
