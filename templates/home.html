Phase I Pilot

The CAHSI-HACU HSI AI Readiness Consortium

AIREX launches with a consortium of 542 Hispanic-Serving Institutions through a partnership between CAHSI and HACU, two organizations that have spent decades strengthening higher education for Hispanic communities.

CAHSI
Computing Alliance of Hispanic-Serving Institutions

A national network dedicated to increasing the number of Hispanics who pursue and complete baccalaureate and graduate degrees in computing.
HACU
Hispanic Association of Colleges and Universities

The leading national association representing Hispanic-Serving Institutions, with more than 500 member colleges and universities across the U.S. and Latin America.
UTEP AAII
Academic Alliance for Artificial Intelligence

Based at the University of Texas at El Paso, AAII leads the design and development of AIREX as a platform for institutional AI readiness.

While this pilot focuses on HSIs, AIREX is designed to serve any academic institution. The framework, tools, and resources are institution-agnostic and open to all.
